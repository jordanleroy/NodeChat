Here is the documentation of the NodeChat
To write to all members connected to NodeChat, type your message and press enter
To write to a specific person : #'personName'
To show a list of all existing groups : --showGroups
To join a group : --join 'groupname'
To write to a specific group : @'groupname' your Message
To list all members of a group : --list 'groupName'
To quit a group : --quit 'groupname'
To quit NodeChat press 'Ctrl+C' or type --quit and press enter
